Yes King

The Phenomenon

Michael Willis Heard, globally recognized by his brand "Yes King" or LoveAndLightTv, was one of the most eccentric and high-energy personalities to emerge from the digital adult media underground. Originally from Glendale, California, Heard previously worked as a pastor in Columbus, Ohio in 2012, and later pursued a PhD in 2020. However, it was his transition into adult content creation that cemented his status as a legendary figure in internet subculture.

Operating out of his LoveAndLightTv hub, Heard became a staple in the "Thugposting" meme universe. He was known for his raw, unpolished, and intensely expressive moaning during explicit scenes. Meme creators isolated his audio to place over unrelated videos on Reddit and TikTok, making his talkative style impossible to ignore.

Ascension & Viral Stardom

Heard's internet ascension truly began in early 2023 when a clip featuring him and another creator went viral. In the video, he delivered his iconic "Yes, King" response, instantly birthing a massive meme trend that echoed the cultural trajectory of other Thugposting legends like Dreamybull.

The original meme was quickly followed by a massive catalog of viral quotes, including the widely misheard "I'm a Lion Pizza Chicken", "Who Made That Mess?", and "You Digging In Me". Beyond his solo clips, he hosted a YouTube video podcast titled Kingz in Konversation with his friends Enrique and Mohka, showing off a deeply engaging and authentic personality beyond the memes.

Final Chapter & Legacy

Tragically, on November 9, 2025, Michael Willis Heard passed away. His daughter announced his death on Facebook, leading to an immediate outpouring of grief from his fans. The news caused a massive surge of tribute posts across TikTok, X, and Reddit, permanently solidifying his legacy as an irreplaceable titan of internet history.
